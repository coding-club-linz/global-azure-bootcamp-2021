Global Azure Austria 2021

Notes from the field Microsoft Information Protection

Friday, April 16, 2021 14:00 - 14:50 CEST

Ever thought about how the different protections play together to protect your information? In this session you will learn how Azure Information Protection, Endpoint DLP, Cloud App Security and Conditional Access can work together to protect every aspect during work. It will also be a part to monitor the solutions with Azure Log Analytics. All of this is showcased with real world examples.

What you will learn: - Integration possibility with other systems - Benefits and Limits of the solutions in production environments - Roadmap for a successful setup
